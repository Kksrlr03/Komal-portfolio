Projects

ProdLoca

ProdLoca – AI‑Powered Local Product Finder

ProdLoca is my attempt to bridge the gap between local retailers and nearby customers. The idea came from a simple problem: people often don't know where to find a product nearby, and local shops struggle to show what they have in stock.

I designed ProdLoca as a platform where shops can update their product availability in real‑time. Customers can search, view locations on a live Mapbox map, and directly chat or place secure in‑app orders — no middlemen.

Stack: React frontend, Firebase realtime DB, Mapbox GL.

Quote Terminals – Autonomous X (Twitter) Quote Bot

I built an autonomous system that posts motivational quotes on X (Twitter) every 5 minutes. It uses GPT‑4 for generation, DistilRoBERTa for sentiment filtering, DynamoDB queueing, and IFTTT for posting.

The project ran successfully for weeks, tweeting fresh AI‑generated or curated quotes. I paused it when IFTTT Premium (~₹200/month) became too costly, but the automation worked flawlessly.
