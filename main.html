ProdLoca – AI‑Powered Local Product Finder

ProdLoca bridges the gap between local retailers and nearby customers. Shops update product availability in real time; customers see inventory on an interactive Mapbox map, chat, and order without any middleman.

Stack: React front‑end, Firebase Realtime DB, and Mapbox GL.

Quote Terminals – Autonomous X (Twitter) Quote Bot

Quote Terminals is an end‑to‑end AI pipeline that posts motivational quotes to X every five minutes — no human touch.

• GPT‑4 generates fresh quotes.
• DistilRoBERTa filters sentiment for positivity.
• SQLite/DynamoDB guarantee uniqueness & queueing.
• IFTTT handles final posting.

Ran flawlessly for weeks; paused only due to IFTTT premium cost. Architecture remains micro‑SaaS ready.
