ProdLoca

ProdLoca – AI-Powered Local Product Finder

ProdLoca is my attempt to bridge the gap between local retailers and nearby customers. The idea came from a simple problem: people often don't know where to find a product nearby, and local shops struggle to show what they have in stock.

I designed ProdLoca as a platform where shops can update their product availability in real-time using a simple dashboard. Customers can search for items, view locations on a live Mapbox map, and directly chat or place secure in-app orders — no middlemen.

It’s built using React for the frontend, Firebase for real-time updates, and Mapbox GL for location mapping. The goal is to support local businesses while giving users a faster, more reliable way to shop locally.

Quote Terminals – Autonomous X (Twitter) Quote Bot

I built an autonomous system that posts motivational quotes on X (Twitter) every 5 minutes. The idea was simple: quotes are a great source of motivation, so what if AI could generate and share them automatically?

I used GPT-4 to generate new quotes and also pulled from sources like Wikipedia and my own SQLite database. Each quote was passed through a sentiment filter using HuggingFace's DistilRoBERTa to ensure it stayed positive and meaningful.

Once a quote was generated, it was checked against existing entries in the SQLite database. If it was unique, it would be posted and stored — preventing any repeats in the future.

The final flow pushed the accepted quote into a DynamoDB queue, which was picked up by an IFTTT trigger that posted it on my X account called Quote Terminals.

The project ran successfully for weeks, tweeting fresh AI-generated or curated quotes at regular intervals. I later paused the project as IFTTT’s premium plan (~₹200/month) became unaffordable, but the automation worked flawlessly end-to-end.
